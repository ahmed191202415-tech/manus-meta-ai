Key Insights

The account is performing well on ad attractiveness, as CTR remains above 5% overall, which is a strong signal for relevance and creative-market fit.

The main gap is not traffic generation; it is conversion consistency across campaigns. Several campaigns generate clicks and engagement, but only a subset converts efficiently into leads.

Result comparison is slightly affected by mixed optimization and measurement patterns across campaigns, including leads, messaging outcomes, and website contact actions.

Recommendations

Increase budget gradually on 'شهر 4' while monitoring lead quality and cost stability.

Scale the stronger lead campaign with 18 leads by cloning its best-performing creatives and audiences rather than applying broad uncontrolled scaling.

Review or pause weaker campaigns such as 'شركة شحن' and the sales-focused campaign unless there is a strategic reason to keep them live.

Standardize success measurement across campaigns so future reports can compare lead generation, messaging, and website outcomes more accurately.

For the next step, inspect performance by ad set and ad creative to identify whether the strongest effect comes from audience targeting, placement, or creative execution.
