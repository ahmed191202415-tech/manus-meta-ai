# Decision Framework

- Scale: reliable tracking, enough sample size, strong conversion economics.
- Hold: mixed evidence, medium confidence, or insufficient sample size.
- Stop: sustained poor economics with reliable tracking.
- Fix Tracking: missing or suspicious conversion data.
- Fix Landing Page: sessions exist but engagement or conversion is weak.
- Fix CTA/Form: engagement is high but conversion is low.
- Fix Mobile UX: mobile conversion materially underperforms desktop.

Every decision should include confidence and missing data.
